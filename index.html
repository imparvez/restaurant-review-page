The Bagel Shop

Carter Road, Bandra West · Café

Love the ambience this cafe offers - open air, very relaxed with beautiful plants. To add to the charm, it is pet friendly and if you are lucky, you will bump into a cute dog to play with :) I've tried the Blueberry muffin and the Classic Club Bagel Sandwich, both of which are yummy! They have a range of stuff on the menu and if you are a Meat lover / Goan / Bandra bugger or equivalent, you will love this place!. Was quite excited about coming here. The milshakes and stuff was amazing. Really good flavour and all. Didn't really like the bagels so much. Definitely not up to the quality. For the price, I would definitely expect MUCH better quality.
